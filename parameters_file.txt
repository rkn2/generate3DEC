	COHESION: 
		
		CONCRETE: 
		
		c is the cohesion and mju is the coefficient of friction that are for various cases as follows:
		c=0.25mPa and mju=0.60 for concrete placed against hardened concrete with surface clean but not intentionally roughened
		c=1.0mPa and mju=1.40 for concrete placed monolithically,
		c=0.0mPa and mju=0.60 for concrete anchored to as-rolled structural steel by headed studs
		and the reference is A23.3 Design of Concrete Structures by Canadian Portland Cement Association 
		SOURCE: https://www.researchgate.net/post/Is_there_any_empirical_equation_for_determining_concrete_cohesion_strength_and_internal_friction_angle
